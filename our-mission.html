Our Mission

Ministry Mission Statement

MCCC is a Mission-driven church, committed to Christ, edifying our Congregation, loving our Community.

Our Ministries:

Exaltation

Let the Triune God enjoy and be glorified through our Sunday worship and other worship services. By doing this, our worship may enhance the spiritual growth of disciples and help the seekers experiencing the presence of God.

Edification

According to the different spiritual needs of seekers, new disciples, growing disciples, ministering and shepherding disciples to teach and communicate effectively the Biblical truth with them, so that they may understand and obey the Biblical truth in their daily life.

Evangelism

To promote and train every single believer to become the witness of Jesus Christ and be fervent to evangelize to the lost souls at the local community.

Caring

To motivate every single believer to participate in people group, in which  a feeling of home and a sense of belonging being cultivated. Moreover, to encourage brothers and sisters to love one another and care for one another, even seekers will feel the acceptance.

Stewardship

Faithfully manage and use the property, resources and money, which God has given us, to glorify His name,and to edify people.

Our Slogan:

You are precious to God!
